Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Road Trip Weather Alert service ("Service") collects, uses, and stores your information.

Information We Collect

  • Phone number: Collected when an administrator adds you as a user, used solely to send SMS weather alerts.
  • GPS location: Submitted by you when you tap "Send My Location." Used to check for active weather alerts near your position.
  • Name: A display name (e.g., "Mom") set by the administrator to identify your account.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you SMS alerts when severe weather is detected near your reported location.
  • To display your last known location and current weather status on your personal page.
  • To send a warning if your location has not been updated in over 12 hours.

SMS Messaging

By participating in this Service, you consent to receive SMS text messages from us regarding weather alerts. Message frequency varies based on active weather conditions. Standard message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to deliver SMS messages via Twilio (our SMS provider) or as required by law.

Data Retention

Your location history and alert records are retained for the duration of the trip and may be deleted by the administrator at any time. You may request deletion by contacting the administrator.

Contact

For questions or to request data deletion, contact the service administrator directly.
